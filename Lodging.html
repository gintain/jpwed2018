Lodging

Jay Peak Resort has various accomodation options. We will provide you with our Group Reservation Number, which ensures a 15% discount on hotel rooms and a 20% discount on cottages and condominiums. Note that the discount is not available online; calling to reserve lodging is required in order for the discount to apply. Staying at the resort is recommended as shuttle service on the day of wedding is available from within the resort. Photos and information of the resort lodging options can be found at the Jay Peak Resort Lodging site. Don't hesitate to contact us if you need the Group Reservation Number.

There are other options outside of the resort that are worth considering. There are hotels and bed and breakfasts in Jay and Montgomery, which is 15 minutes away from Jay.
