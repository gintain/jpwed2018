Lodging

Jay Peak Resort has various accomodation options. Please use our group reservation number, 632606, to obtain a 15% discount on hotel rooms and 25% discount on cottages and condominiums. Please call the Jay Peak reservation desk at 1-800-451-4449 to use the group reservation number, though success has been reported using the reservation number online. We recommend staying at the resort to utilize the shuttle service provided during the wedding. Photos and information of the resort lodging options can be found at the Jay Peak Resort Lodging site. See below for a pricing guide. Note that all prices are subject to a 9% VT state tax and $26/night resort fee, not reflected below.

There are other options outside of the resort that are worth considering. There are hotels and bed and breakfasts in Jay and Montgomery, which is 15 minutes away from Jay.
