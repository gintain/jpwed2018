Destination: Jay Peak Resort

Located just ten miles (driving distance) from the Canadian border, reaching Jay Peak Resort requires a bit of driving. Listed below are the nearest airports.

The Burlington International Airport (IATA airport code BTV) is about 1.5 hours from Jay. More information can be found at the airport's website.

The nearest large metropolitan area is Montreal, Quebec, which is about 1.75 hours from Jay. While you can rent a car and cross the border, be sure to research any fees or taxes for doing so. It may be preferable to take a Greyhound bus to Burlington, VT, and rent there. The larger airport is the Montreal/Pierre Elliott Trudeau Internation Airport (IATA airport code YUL) More information can be found here.

The nearest large metropolitan area in the US is Boston, MA, at a scenic 3.5 hours away. Logan International Airport's IATA code is BOS.

New York, NY, is an incredibly scenic 6.25 hours away. There are three large airports to choose from (IATA code provided in parentheses). Fly to John F Kennedy International Airport (JFK), LaGuardia Airport (LGA), or Newark International Airport (EWR).

Finally, don't forget to check some of the smaller airports in the area for potential deals. Some airports to consider are Manchester-Boston Regional Airport (MHT) in Manchester, NH, TF Green Aiport (PVD) in Providence, RI, Bradley International Airport (BDL) in Hartford, CT, Portland International Jetport (PWM) in Portland, ME, Albany International Airport (ALB) in Albany, NY, and Westchester County Airport (HPN) in White Plains, NY.
